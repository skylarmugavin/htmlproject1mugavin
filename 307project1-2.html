Packing List

Use this checklist to think through what you need to bring.

Clothing

  • Tops
  • Bottoms
  • Sleepwear
  • Underwear and socks
  • Shoes

Toiletries

  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Hair products
  • Skincare
  • Medications

Essentials

  • Phone and charger
  • Wallet or purse
  • Travel documents
  • Reusable water bottle

Packing Tips

  • Lay everything out before putting it in your bag.
  • Roll clothes to save space.
  • Pack essentials in a carry-on if flying.
